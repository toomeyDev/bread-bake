Whole Wheat Bread

Description

This easy whole-wheat bread is simple to make and relatively healthy. With only 6 ingredients, you can have a fresh loaf of bread in only 2 hours!

Recipe can easily be doubled if needed, bread can be stored long-term in the freezer for later use.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1/4 ounce active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, presoftened
  • 3 3/4 to 4 cups whole wheat flour (varies)
  • 2/3 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F / 176C.
  2. Place warm water in a large bowl, sprinkle yeast into water. Let sit for 5 minutes to allow proofing.
  3. Add honey and butter to yeast mixture.
  4. Blend in 2 cups of flour and salt until moist, at a medium speed.
  5. Stir in rest of flour slowly, until dough does not stick to sides of mixing bowl.
  6. Knead dough on floured board or surface, adding more flour as needed until dough is smooth, about 8-10 minutes.
  7. Place dough in large greased or oiled bowl,cover with a damp towl and let rise in warm area until doubled (around 30 minutes)
  8. Punch down the dough and shape it into a loaf shape, place in greased 9x5 loaf pan, let rise covered for 30 more minutes.
  9. Place uncovered loaf in oven and bake for around 40 minutes or until knife inserted into center comes out clean.

Keep on countertop wrapped in foil for up to 4 days, or in fridge for up to 5. Freeze as soon as cooled for long-term storage.
